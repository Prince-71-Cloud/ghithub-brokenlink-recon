# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who audit public GitHub repositories for broken links, usually working from a username or organization target and reviewing source files one repo at a time.

## Product Purpose

Help the user scan public GitHub source trees for link health, surface valid, broken, and review-needed URLs clearly, and keep the results exportable for follow-up work.

## Brand Personality

Technical, focused, reliable.

The interface should feel precise, transparent, and calm under load.

## Anti-references

- No generic SaaS dashboard chrome.
- No noisy marketing copy or decorative clutter.
- No hidden state changes or ambiguous scan status.
- No overly playful visuals that distract from the review workflow.

## Design Principles

- Make scan state obvious at all times.
- Keep the review sheet readable at a glance.
- Surface uncertainty instead of forcing false certainty.
- Favor compact controls that support fast filtering and export.
- Preserve the source data model in the UI so users can trust what they are seeing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for strong contrast, keyboard-friendly controls, and clear focus states.
Keep motion subtle and non-blocking, and make the scan results usable without relying on color alone.
